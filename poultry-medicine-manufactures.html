An Exclusive Global Position Among Leading Poultry Medicine Manufacturers

PVS Group has secured a leading position as the top poultry medicine manufacturer in India, owing to its extensive support to the poultry industry over the past 25 years. As a prominent supplier of premium poultry treatment products, Q9 International has made a significant impact with its innovative formulations, offering vitamins, minerals, amino acids, probiotics, prebiotics, enzymes, herbs, chemicals, and antibiotics (in the form of oral tablets, bolus, liquids, and powders). This has established the company’s unique reputation internationally among poultry medicine manufacturers. Q9 International provides a comprehensive range of solutions for poultry diseases, including antibiotics (liquids), antibiotics (water-soluble powders), anticoccidials, and dewormers. The company’s expertly designed poultry treatment products ensure the health and well-being of your poultry at all times.

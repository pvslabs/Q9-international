Delivering Exceptional Animal Health Care Solutions Since 1993

Q9 International's parent company, PVS Group, was founded in 1993 by Dr. Dr. Pamulapati Venkata Seshaiah, who began as a distributor of Indovax products. The following year, he established ANU LABS, India's first specialized laboratory dedicated to disease diagnostics and feed analysis for aquaculture and poultry. Recognizing the growing need for high-quality aqua healthcare products to enhance shrimp and fish farming, Dr. PV Seshaiah set up the first manufacturing unit for aqua healthcare products in 1997. This venture quickly grew into one of the leading manufacturers and exporters in the aqua and animal healthcare industry. With manufacturing as its foundation, Q9 International delivers innovative, high-quality solutions tailored to the evolving needs of animal healthcare. Leveraging extensive experience and strong technical expertise, the company effectively identifies and addresses industry challenges. As one of the most successful animal health pharmaceutical companies in Vijayawada, Q9 International has expanded its operations to multiple international markets.

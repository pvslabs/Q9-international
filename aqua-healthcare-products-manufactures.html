Defining Quality: As a Leading Supplier of Shrimp & Fish Healthcare Products

Q9 International, a reputed name in the aquaculture industry, is one of the leading aquaculture probiotics manufacturers in India. Backed by over a decade of research and the PVS Group’s legacy in animal healthcare since 1993, the company excels in developing effective shrimp healthcare products, especially for white gut disease. Known for its strong field knowledge and innovative approach, Q9 supports healthier aquaculture while boosting client success. Based in Mangalagiri, India, the company offers a wide range of high-performance solutions. These include concentrated probiotics with enzymes, herbal remedies, biological pond management solutions, and bio-degradant bacteria. Their product lineup also features water softeners cum gas absorbents, pH regulators, herbal lice eliminators, broad-spectrum sanitizers, brominated compounds, and algicides. Q9 is a trusted manufacturer of fish and shrimp immunomodulators.

As a leading supplier of fish disease solutions in India, Q9 brings extensive experience and expertise in producing highly effective treatments for white gut disease in shrimp. Backed by deep research and a solid foundation in aquaculture science, Q9’s formulations are trusted by customers across India and internationally. The company has established itself as a reputable name among aquaculture probiotics manufacturers in India. With a commitment to innovation and sustainability, Q9 continues to set new benchmarks in aquaculture health management.

Q9 International offers a comprehensive range of premium healthcare products for fish, including highly concentrated probiotics with enzymes, biological solutions for pond management, high-performance water softeners with gas-absorbing properties, pH regulators, herbal lice eliminators, broad-spectrum sanitizers, disinfectants, brominated compounds, algicides, and more. Each product is designed to support effective disease prevention and promote the healthy development of aquaculture systems.

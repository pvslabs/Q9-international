Strengthening society through impactful corporate responsibility.

Since 1993, PVS Group has been a pioneer in animal healthcare, consistently giving back to the community through impactful Corporate Social Responsibility initiatives and social development efforts. Its offshoot, Q9 International, carries this legacy forward with equal dedication. To make a real difference at the grassroots level, PVS Group introduced ANU Labs—a unique organization in India that supports local farmers by offering soil and pond water testing services. Through its “Disease Diagnostic Research & Feed Analytical Laboratory,” ANU Labs also provides essential medicines at discounted rates and promotes the use of sustainable technologies for long-term economic, biological, and environmental benefits. Guided by a vision to bring positive change, Q9 International is driven by the belief that all animals deserve access to the best healthcare products and growth opportunities.

Q9 International is one of India’s most trustworthy animal medicine manufacturers. Its growth is attributed to the dynamic leadership of Dr. Pamulapati Venkata Seshaiah and the business expertise and collaborations inherited from PVS Group.

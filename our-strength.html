Dr. Seshaiah V. Pamulapati,
the secret to PVS group’s success

Q9 International, one of the premier animal medicine manufacturers in India, is part of the esteemed PVS Group—renowned for its excellence in animal health products. The driving force behind the Group's success is the visionary leadership of Dr. Pamulapati Venkata Seshaiah (Dr. PVS), who was born in 1965 in C.S. Puram, Prakasam District. Coming from a modest background, Dr. PVS began working in his teens to support his family, eventually serving as an Assistant Veterinary Surgeon. In 1993, he left government service to pursue his entrepreneurial aspirations, starting as a poultry vaccine distributor for INDOVAX. His commitment to innovation led to the establishment of ANU LABS in 1994, a pioneering venture in water and soil testing for aquaculture—marking the beginning of a legacy in animal health and wellness.

Dr. PVS, with his sound technical knowledge, eagerness to explore, focused approach, and dedication to achieving objectives, has consistently reached one milestone after another, establishing himself as a leading industrialist in the region. His entrepreneurial skills, including innovative thinking, sharp observation, and clear analysis of business situations, combined with sheer hard work, have been key to his success. As a natural leader, he values his employees as family and fosters a work environment where they feel respected and supported. His brainchild, International Health Care, is recognized as a trustworthy employer in the animal and aqua healthcare sectors, offering equal opportunities to all.

PVS Group: A Leader in Animal Healthcare and Feed Supplements

Under Dr. PVS’ leadership, which fosters exceptional employee engagement at PVS Group, the talented team looks beyond the platform to offer comprehensive solutions across the entire animal healthcare sector. With 553 employees across divisions like production, administration, accounts, marketing, and sales, PVS Group is driven by a powerful blend of technical experts from diverse backgrounds and world-class infrastructure. This combination propels PVS toward becoming a recognized leader in the aqua and animal health industries. Over the years, PVS has built a strong reputation for delivering safe, reliable, and high-quality feed supplements to an ever-expanding global customer base. That’s why, when it comes to animal health pharmaceuticals, Q9 International is beyond compare.
